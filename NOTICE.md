# NOTICE — Immutable Fact AI / The Real Bot

## Project identity and provenance

This repository, **`immutable-fact-ai`**, is the technical / architectural
side of a single body of work by **Carey James Balboa** under
**IT Help San Diego Inc.** The companion public work is the manifesto-style
essay **"The Real Bot Hasn't Been Built Yet"** (also referred to in this
repository as *The Real Bot* and discussed in the gap analysis document).

The two are intentionally linked:

- **The Real Bot** is the public-facing argument: why current generative AI
  systems are mislabeled "Scholar Bots" when they are in fact "Creative
  Bots", and what a verified, foundation-gated AI system would have to do
  to deserve the "Scholar" name.
- **Immutable Fact AI** is the protected product-path / architectural
  effort to build that system.

Together, they are a **commercial / product-path asset** of Carey James
Balboa and IT Help San Diego Inc.
